# Camera
* Orbit: Cmd+B
* Pan: Cmd+R

# Tools
* Select: Cmd+/ (or space?)
* Push/Pull: Cmd+=
* Erase: E
* Move: Cmd+0
* Scale: Cmd+9
* Line: L

# measurement textfield
In the statusbar on the right bottom of the Window there's a measurement textfield. You can just type numbers without having focus on the textfield.
